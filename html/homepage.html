Natural Ghee in India

Ghee, also known as clarified butter, has been a staple in Indian households for centuries, revered not only for its rich flavour but also for its numerous health benefits. Made by simmering butter until the milk solids separate and then straining them out, natural ghee is a versatile ingredient that holds a special place in Indian cuisine and Ayurvedic medicine and these are the benefits:

Nutritional Powerhouse
Rich Source of Healthy Fats
Boosts Digestion
Enhances Flavour and Texture
Supports Weight Management
Promotes Detoxification

About Us

    Our Farm is a Bio-diverse, Organic ecosystem, certified by ECOCERT and located in the beautiful village near Bangalore. Our work is inspired by ancient wisdom and sustainability. Every time you buy from us, you help pull back carbon from the atmosphere into the soil, secure rural livelihoods and empower women in our villages.

     We're passionate about providing you with the finest organic ghee and tomatoes, crafted with care and dedication. Our organic ghee is more than just clarified butter; it's a celebration of purity, flavour, and health because no chemical added while growing and after harvesting.

Why Choose Organic Ghee?

     Experience the Difference: Our organic ghee is sourced from the purest organic milk, free from synthetic hormones, antibiotics, and GMOs. With a commitment to organic farming practices, we ensure that every spoonful of our ghee is brimming with natural goodness.
